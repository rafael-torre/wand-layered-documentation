# Design Exploration: [Feature or Area Name]

A record of the design directions explored before committing to a final approach. Use this during the iterative phase when multiple approaches are being evaluated — it captures the "why this direction over others" that disappears once the final mockup exists and intermediate frames are cleaned up.

Not every feature needs a formal exploration record. Use this template when the team genuinely evaluated multiple directions, when a non-obvious choice was made, or when a stakeholder or client was involved in a direction decision.
