# Raw Inputs — Layer 0: Business

Materials gathered, not authored. Work through this checklist during client onboarding to capture the business context needed for synthesis. Not every item will be available for every engagement — gather what exists and note what's missing.

---

## Client-Provided


| Input                                    | Description                                                           | Who Provides             |
| ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Onboarding decks / company presentations | How the client describes themselves, their products, and their market | Client stakeholder       |
| Marketing materials / brand guidelines   | Tone, positioning, how they present to their customers                | Client marketing team    |
| Existing systems documentation           | Architecture diagrams, API docs, vendor information, internal tools   | Client technical team    |
| Org charts or team structures            | Relevant teams, reporting lines, key roles                            | Client stakeholder       |
| Contracts / SOWs / MSAs                  | Scope, budget, timeline, legal constraints, deliverables              | Client / Engagement Lead |


## Discovery


| Input                                            | Description                                                     | Who Provides         |
| ------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Discovery call recordings and notes              | Initial conversations covering goals, pain points, expectations | PM / Engagement Lead |
| Stakeholder interview recordings and transcripts | Individual conversations with key stakeholders                  | PM / Engagement Lead |


## Research

| Industry reports and market research | Market size, trends, regulatory landscape           | PM / Research |
| Competitor websites and products     | Competitor offerings, positioning, reviews          | PM / Research |
