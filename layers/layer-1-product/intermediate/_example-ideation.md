# Ideation

Brainstorming output for a product area or theme. Used during early discovery when the team is identifying which problems are worth solving and generating initial ideas for how to address them. This is upstream of problem statements and solution explorations — it captures the broad exploration before the team commits to specific problems or approaches.

Not every feature goes through a formal ideation step. Use this template when starting from a vague or open-ended brief (e.g., "improve customer retention") that needs to be broken down into concrete problem areas before feature specs can be written.
